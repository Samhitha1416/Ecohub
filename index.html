About this page

Our mission is to provide organizations, recycling facilities, and regulatory bodies with a powerful platform to manage e-waste effectively, enhance transparency, and reduce environmental impact. By leveraging technology and collaboration, we aim to create a greener, cleaner future for generations to come.

Services we offer

  • User Accounts: Create separate accounts for Pollution Control Boards (PCBs), Recycling Facilities, and Organizations.
  • Monitor and Analytics: Real-time monitoring and analytics for PCBs to track e-waste disposal trends.
  • Trade Options: Facilitate buying and selling of electronic waste between Recycling Facilities and Organizations.
  • Inventory Management:Advanced inventory management for tracking e-waste throughout its lifecycle.
